## Research Methodology

Keyword research was conducted based on:
1. Analysis of the live site's content and positioning
2. Topic clustering around core business pillars (Build, Fund, Operate)
3. Search intent mapping for investor, community, and operator audiences
4. Local SEO keyword identification for each facility location
5. Competitive keyword inference from the freestanding ER and healthcare infrastructure sectors
6. Long-tail opportunity identification from FAQ content and service descriptions
