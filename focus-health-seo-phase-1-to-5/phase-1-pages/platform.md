## Internal Linking Observations

- Links to `/partners` and `/track-record`
- No links to `/investors`, `/our-process`, `/insights`, `/leadership`, or any facility pages
- Thin internal linking for a core pillar page

---

## UX Observations

- Visually impressive with animated diagram and video
- Text content is sparse — the page communicates primarily through visuals
- No scrollable content sections that explain the platform methodology in detail
- Users looking for substantive platform information may find the page insufficient

---

## Content Observations

- Extremely light on text — estimated 200–300 words of visible copy
- No detailed explanation of how each platform phase (Build, Fund, Operate) works
- No case studies or examples illustrating the platform in action
- No mention of technology, compliance frameworks, or operational systems
- No comparison with alternative models (e.g., hospital-affiliated ERs, chain ERs)
- No social proof or partner endorsements on this page

---

## SEO Observations

- **Title tag:** "Platform" — extremely weak; no keywords, no brand, no context
- **Meta description:** Good — mentions "build, fund, and operate platform", "institutional-grade healthcare infrastructure", "Texas"
- **H1:** "Build + Fund + Operate" — short and branded but lacks keyword context like "healthcare infrastructure platform"
- **Structured data:** WebPageStructuredData only
- **Canonical:** `/platform`
- Content-to-code ratio is very low due to heavy reliance on video and animation
- No secondary headings (H2s) with keyword-rich text

---

## Metadata Observations

- Title tag is only 8 characters — well under optimal length (50–60 characters)
- Meta description is adequate at ~130 characters
- Missing keywords in title severely limits SERP visibility

---

## What Is Missing

1. A keyword-rich title tag (e.g., "Healthcare Infrastructure Platform | Build, Fund & Operate | Focus Health")
2. Detailed text content explaining each platform phase
3. Internal links to facility pages as platform examples
4. Links to `/our-process` for operational detail
5. Links to `/insights` for supporting content
6. Case study references or track record proof
7. Partner/investor testimonials
8. Trust signals (certifications, partnerships, awards)
9. FAQ section targeting platform-related queries

---

## What Should Be Improved

1. **Title tag:** Expand to include primary keywords and brand name
2. **H1:** Expand to "Build, Fund, and Operate Healthcare Infrastructure" or similar
3. **Body content:** Add 800–1,200 words of detailed platform methodology text across three sections (Build, Fund, Operate)
4. **Internal linking:** Add links to at least five other internal pages
5. **Social proof:** Add a testimonial or partnership badge section
6. **Schema:** Consider adding `Service` schema for the platform offering
7. **Visual fallback:** Ensure the animated diagram has alt text and a static fallback for crawlers
