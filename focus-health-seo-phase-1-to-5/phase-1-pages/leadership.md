## SEO Observations

- **Title tag:** "Leadership" — weak; 10 characters, no keywords or brand
- **Meta description:** Strong — mentions "experienced leadership team", "$100M+ in healthcare revenue", "24+ facilities", "freestanding ER expertise"
- **H1:** "Empowering Healthcare Through Visionary Leadership" — aspirational but keyword-light
- **Structured data:** WebPageStructuredData only — no Person schema for individual leaders
- **Canonical:** `/leadership`
- No `Person` or `Organization` → `member` structured data

---

## Metadata Observations

- Title tag needs expansion with keywords and brand
- Meta description is data-driven and effective
- Missing Person schema is a significant E-E-A-T opportunity

---

## What Is Missing

1. Keyword-rich title tag (e.g., "Leadership Team | Healthcare Operations Experts | Focus Health")
2. `Person` schema for each featured leader (name, jobTitle, worksFor, sameAs LinkedIn)
3. Links to `/investors` and `/insights`
4. Advisory board section (if applicable)
5. Team achievement highlights or milestones
6. Individual leader pages or anchor links for direct linking

---

## What Should Be Improved

1. **Title tag:** Expand to include "healthcare leadership team" keywords
2. **H1:** Consider "Meet the Healthcare Leadership Team Behind Focus Health"
3. **Person schema:** Add structured data for each featured leader with LinkedIn sameAs
4. **Internal linking:** Add links to investors page and relevant insight articles
5. **Achievement callouts:** Add specific operational milestones next to leader bios
6. **Image alt text:** Ensure all headshots have descriptive alt attributes including name and role
