The Texas freestanding emergency room (FSER) market opportunity — population growth, geographic gaps, and facility demand.

- "Our Platform" → `/platform`

---

## Internal Linking Observations

- Links to `/partners` and `/platform` only
- No links to `/track-record`, `/investors`, `/insights`, `/leadership`, or facility pages
- Very limited internal linking for a page positioned as a market-opportunity hub

---

## UX Observations

- Texas SVG map is visually engaging but contains minimal actionable data
- Market driver cards are very brief — each contains one to two sentences
- No data tables, charts, or detailed statistics to support market claims
- Page feels like an overview slide rather than a comprehensive market analysis

---

## Content Observations

- **Thin content** — estimated 150–200 words of visible body text
- No specific data points (population figures, FSER count, revenue data, growth rates)
- No competitive landscape analysis
- No regulatory environment discussion
- No mention of specific Texas markets beyond the three map clusters
- No supporting citations or sources for market claims
- No mention of micro-hospitals or wellness clinics as market opportunities
- The $5B+ industry reference in the meta description does not appear in the body content

---

## SEO Observations

- **Title tag:** "Market" — extremely weak; 6 characters, no keywords or brand
- **Meta description:** Strong — mentions "freestanding emergency room market", "population growth", "$5B+ industry"
- **H1:** "Why Texas FSERs" — informal, uses abbreviation, lacks full keyword context
- **Structured data:** WebPageStructuredData only
- **Canonical:** `/market`
- SVG map content is not crawlable as text
- No H2 or H3 headings with keyword-rich text visible in the page structure

---

## Metadata Observations

- Title tag is only 6 characters — critically under-optimised
- Meta description is well-written at ~135 characters
- The mismatch between the rich meta description and thin body content may affect quality signals

---

## What Is Missing

1. A keyword-rich title tag (e.g., "Texas Healthcare Market Opportunity | Freestanding ERs & Micro-Hospitals | Focus Health")
2. Detailed market data — population statistics, FSER counts, revenue projections
3. Sections on individual metro markets (DFW, Houston, Austin–San Antonio)
4. Regulatory environment overview for Texas healthcare
5. Competitive landscape analysis
6. Internal links to track record, facility pages, investors, and insights
7. Supporting insight article links (e.g., "Why Texas Is a Prime Market for FSERs")
8. Data visualisations with crawlable fallback text
9. Downloadable market report or investor deck CTA

---

## What Should Be Improved

1. **Title tag:** Expand to 50–60 characters with primary keywords
2. **H1:** Change to "Texas Freestanding Emergency Room Market Opportunity" or similar
3. **Body content:** Add 1,000–1,500 words covering market data, metro breakdowns, competitive landscape, and growth projections
4. **Internal linking:** Add links to at least six other pages including facility examples and insight articles
5. **Data presentation:** Add tables or charts with real market statistics
6. **Schema:** Consider `Dataset` or `Article` schema if presenting research data
7. **Metro sub-sections:** Add H2 sections for each target market (DFW, Houston, Austin–San Antonio) as stepping stones toward dedicated market detail pages
