# Page Audit: Partners

**URL:** https://www.getfocushealth.com/partners  
**Page Type:** Conversion / Lead Generation  
**Status:** Live  
**Priority Level:** Critical  

---

## Current H1

"Business Opportunities"

---

## Summary of Current Content

1. **PageHero** — Title "Business Opportunities" with value proposition text about healthcare real estate investment.
2. **Investment Highlights** — Four cards: Tangible Assets, Experienced Operators, Proven Strategy, Turnkey Solutions.
3. **Partnership Models** — Two pathways: Turn-Key ER Ownership and Management Support Model, each with detailed descriptions.
4. **Investment Inquiry Form** (id: `opportunity-form`) — Fields: first name, last name, email, phone, market interest, cash to invest ($250K–$5M+), partner type (Physician/Investor/Other), additional information. Submits to `/api/submissions/partner`.
5. **Due Diligence Resources** — Supporting information section.

---

## Current Primary Topic

Investment and partnership opportunities in healthcare infrastructure — converting qualified investor and community leads.

---

## Current CTA

- "Submit Request" (form submission)
- Internal links to `/platform`, `/track-record`, `/leadership`

---

## Internal Linking Observations

- Links to `/platform`, `/track-record`, `/leadership`
- No links to `/investors`, `/insights`, `/market`, `/our-process`, or facility pages
- The anchor `#opportunity-form` is the primary deep-link target from across the site

---

## UX Observations

- Clear conversion funnel with prominent form
- Partnership model descriptions help users self-qualify
- Form fields are appropriate for lead qualification (cash range, partner type)
- Page is well-structured for conversion but could benefit from more trust elements near the form
- No privacy notice or data handling statement near the form

---

## Content Observations

- Partnership models are clearly differentiated
- Investment highlights provide good proof points
- Due diligence section adds credibility
- Missing: testimonials from existing partners
- Missing: FAQ section addressing common investor queries
- Missing: timeline or process overview for new partners
- Missing: expected returns or investment structure detail (this detail exists on the Investors page instead)

---

## SEO Observations

- **Title tag:** "Partners" — weak; 8 characters, no keywords or brand
- **Meta description:** Good — mentions "healthcare infrastructure investment", "community healthcare development", "turnkey emergency room management", "Texas"
- **H1:** "Business Opportunities" — generic; does not contain healthcare or investment keywords
- **Structured data:** WebPageStructuredData only
- **Canonical:** `/partners`
- This is the single most important conversion page — it is the destination for nearly every CTA across the site

---

## Metadata Observations

- Title tag needs urgent expansion
- Meta description is keyword-rich and effective
- H1 should align with search intent for "healthcare investment opportunities" or "ER franchise opportunities"

---

## What Is Missing

1. Keyword-rich title tag (e.g., "Healthcare Investment Opportunities | Partner With Focus Health")
2. A keyword-aligned H1 (e.g., "Healthcare Infrastructure Investment Opportunities")
3. Testimonials from existing investors or community partners
4. FAQ section targeting investor and partnership queries
5. Process timeline for new partners
6. Privacy/data handling notice near the form
7. Links to `/investors`, `/insights`, `/market`, and facility pages
8. Schema: consider `InvestmentOrFund` or `Offer` structured data

---

## What Should Be Improved

1. **Title tag:** Expand to include "healthcare investment" and brand
2. **H1:** Replace "Business Opportunities" with a keyword-rich heading
3. **Trust signals near form:** Add partner count, success metrics, or testimonial quote directly above the form
4. **Internal linking:** Add contextual links to investors page, market page, and supporting insight articles
5. **Form UX:** Add a brief privacy statement below the form
6. **FAQ:** Add 5–8 questions targeting "how to invest in healthcare", "ER franchise cost", "healthcare real estate investment"
