# Page Audit: Contact

**URL:** https://www.getfocushealth.com/contact  
**Page Type:** Conversion / Support  
**Status:** Live  
**Priority Level:** Medium  

---

## Current H1

"Contact Us"

---

## Summary of Current Content

1. **PageHero** — Title "Contact Us".
2. **Contact Form** — Fields: name, email, role (Investor/Community/Operator/Media/Other), message. Submits to `/api/submissions/contact`.
3. **Contact Information** — Email: info@getfocushealth.com, Location: "Texas, USA".
4. **Scheduling Widget** — Embedded Google Calendar iframe for booking meetings.

---

## Current Primary Topic

Contact and enquiry submission for partnership, investment, and general queries.

---

## Current CTA

- "Send Message" (form submission)
- Google Calendar scheduling link

---

## Internal Linking Observations

- No internal links from the body content to other pages
- No contextual navigation to related pages (partners, investors, leadership)
- This is a terminal page with no pathways back into the site

---

## UX Observations

- Clean and functional layout
- Google Calendar embed provides a tangible next step beyond the form
- Contact information is minimal — only email and "Texas, USA"
- No physical address shown (the footer address 3001 Skyway Cir N, Irving, TX 75038 is not referenced on this page)
- No phone number displayed
- No expected response time indicated

---

## Content Observations

- **Thin content** — estimated 60–80 words of body text
- No introductory paragraph explaining who should contact and why
- No FAQ section addressing common enquiries
- No mention of specific departments or contact purposes
- No office hours or response time expectations
- No map or directions
- The physical address in the footer (3001 Skyway Cir N, Irving, TX 75038) should appear prominently on this page

---

## SEO Observations

- **Title tag:** "Contact" — weak; 7 characters, no keywords or brand
- **Meta description:** Adequate — mentions "partnership opportunities", "investment inquiries", "community healthcare development", "Texas"
- **H1:** "Contact Us" — generic, no keyword differentiation
- **Structured data:** WebPageStructuredData only — no `ContactPage` or `LocalBusiness` schema
- **Canonical:** `/contact`
- Extremely low text content for indexing

---

## Metadata Observations

- Title tag needs expansion (e.g., "Contact Focus Health | Healthcare Partnership & Investment Enquiries")
- Meta description is competent
- Missing LocalBusiness or ContactPage schema

---

## What Is Missing

1. Keyword-rich title tag
2. Physical address prominently displayed
3. Phone number
4. Introductory paragraph with keywords
5. FAQ section for common enquiries
6. Office hours or response timeframes
7. Map embed or directions link
8. Internal links to partners, investors, and leadership pages
9. `ContactPage` or `LocalBusiness` schema
10. Trust signals (privacy note near form, partnership count)

---

## What Should Be Improved

1. **Title tag:** Expand to include keywords and brand
2. **H1:** Consider "Contact Focus Health — Partnership & Investment Enquiries"
3. **Address:** Add the Irving, TX office address prominently in the body content
4. **Phone number:** Display a contact phone number if available
5. **Body content:** Add 200–300 words of introductory and contextual text
6. **Internal linking:** Add links to `/partners`, `/investors`, `/leadership`
7. **FAQ:** Add 4–6 questions about partnerships, investment process, facility enquiries
8. **Schema:** Add ContactPage or LocalBusiness schema with full address and contact details
9. **Form UX:** Add privacy statement and expected response time below the form
