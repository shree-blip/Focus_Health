## 5. Content Cannibalisation Risks

| Risk | Pages Involved | Overlapping Topic |
|------|---------------|-------------------|
| **High** | `/platform` vs `/our-process` | Both describe the turnkey development model |
| **Medium** | `/partners` vs `/investors` | Both target healthcare investment enquiries |
| **Medium** | Homepage investment section vs `/investors` | Both present the $3.4M raise details |
| **Low** | Track Record process diagram vs `/our-process` | Both show the development process steps |

### Resolution Strategy

| Pair | Differentiation |
|------|----------------|
| Platform vs Our Process | Platform = what the model is (positioning). Our Process = how it works step-by-step (methodology with examples). |
| Partners vs Investors | Partners = conversion page for all partner types (form-first). Investors = education and pitch for investor audience (content-first). |
| Homepage vs Investors | Homepage = overview teaser with CTA to full page. Investors = comprehensive pitch. |
