## Implementation Notes

- Use descriptive, keyword-rich anchor text — avoid "click here", "learn more", or "read more" as standalone anchors
- Place links within body content paragraphs where possible, not just in dedicated link sections
- Each link should feel natural in context — do not force links into unrelated sections
- After implementation, verify all links resolve correctly (no 404s)
- Track the number of internal links per page in a spreadsheet to monitor equity distribution
- Target: every core page should have at least 5 inbound internal links and 5 outbound internal links
