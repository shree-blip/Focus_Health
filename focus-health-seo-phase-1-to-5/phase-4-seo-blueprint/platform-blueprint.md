## Search Intent

Commercial — operators, investors, and partners evaluating Focus Health's platform model. Users want to understand what the platform offers and how it differs from alternatives.

Explain the Build + Fund + Operate platform in detail. Differentiate from competitors. Convert interest into enquiries via links to Partners/Investors.
