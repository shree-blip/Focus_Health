# SEO Blueprint: Contact

**URL:** `/contact`  

---

## Keywords

| Role | Keyword |
|------|---------|
| Primary | contact Focus Health |
| Secondary | Focus Health phone number, Focus Health headquarters, healthcare company contact |
| Long-tail | how to contact Focus Health Irving Texas |

---

## Search Intent

Navigational / Transactional — users who already know Focus Health and need to reach the team.

---

## Page Purpose

Provide clear, immediate contact options and capture enquiries. Reinforce local presence with a verified address and map.

---

## Recommended H1

"Contact Focus Health"

---

## Recommended Title Tag

"Contact Focus Health | Headquarters in Irving, Texas"  
(53 characters)

---

## Recommended Meta Description

"Reach Focus Health at our Irving, Texas headquarters. Contact us for healthcare partnership, investment, or general enquiries."  
(127 characters)

---

## Recommended URL Slug

`/contact` (no change)

---

## Recommended Content Sections

1. **Hero** — H1, one-sentence intro
2. **Contact Details** (new) — Address, phone, email in structured format
3. **Contact Form** — Existing form (retain)
4. **Google Map Embed** (new) — Interactive map showing 3001 Skyway Cir N, Irving, TX 75038
5. **Office Hours** (new) — Business hours for the headquarters
6. **Quick Links** (new) — "Looking for something else?" links to `/partners`, `/investors`, `/insights`, `/facilities/er-of-irving`

## Schema

- WebPage (existing)
- ContactPage type (upgrade from generic WebPage)

## Notes

- Currently ~70 words of body content — critically thin. Adding contact details, office hours, map, and quick links section raises word count to 200+ and adds unique, indexable content
- The structured contact details (address, phone, email) should use Schema.org `contactPoint` markup
- Map embed adds visual engagement and helps confirm the Irving, TX NAP for local SEO
- This page should be accessible from every page via footer and navigation (already in footer, confirm nav)
