# SEO Blueprint: Insights Hub

**URL:** `/insights`  

---

## Keywords

| Role | Keyword |
|------|---------|
| Primary | healthcare infrastructure insights |
| Secondary | freestanding ER industry trends, healthcare investment research, emergency room market analysis |
| Long-tail | Texas freestanding ER market trends 2025, healthcare infrastructure investment insights |

---

## Search Intent

Informational — professionals, investors, and journalists seeking expert perspectives on healthcare infrastructure.

---

## Page Purpose

Content hub and index page. Organise all insight articles by topic cluster, demonstrate thought leadership, and distribute link equity to individual posts.

---

## Recommended H1

"Healthcare Infrastructure Insights"

---

## Recommended Title Tag

"Healthcare Infrastructure Insights | Focus Health Research & Analysis"  
(67 characters)

---

## Recommended Meta Description

"Expert insights on freestanding emergency rooms, healthcare investment, and facility operations from the Focus Health platform team."  
(133 characters)

---

## Recommended URL Slug

`/insights` (no change)

---

## Recommended Content Sections

1. **Hub Hero** — H1, brief intro paragraph (75–100 words) explaining the scope of insights
2. **Featured Insight** — Latest or highest-value article card with image, excerpt, and link
3. **Topic Clusters** (new) — Grouped article listings under cluster headings:
   - Market Intelligence
   - Investor Education
   - Operations & Readiness
   - Company Updates
4. **Article Grid** — All posts with title, category, date, excerpt, and link
5. **Newsletter CTA** (new) — "Subscribe for updates" email capture
6. **Related Resources** (new) — Links to `/platform`, `/market`, `/partners`

---

## CTA Direction

- Primary: Read individual posts
- Secondary: Newsletter subscribe
- Tertiary: "Partner with us"

---

## Trust Elements

- Article count and freshness (display total posts and last-updated date)
- Topic cluster organisation
- Author attribution on cards (add)

---

## Schema

- CollectionPage (existing)
- ItemList (existing) — ensure each post is a ListItem with position, name, url

---

## Notes

- Currently displays cards in a flat grid. Cluster grouping adds topical authority signals
- The insights hub must link to every published insight post — no orphan articles
- Adding a 75–100-word introductory paragraph provides unique body content for the index page (currently thin)
- Newsletter CTA creates a new lead capture channel beyond the partner form
- Every body-content page on the site should link to `/insights` at least once — currently zero pages do so from body content
